About

I'm a full stack web development student and content writer. I began designing websites at the turn of the century, but a full-time detour into retail management saw me fall way behind in maintaining the skills needed in the rapidly changing field of web development.


Since leaving the retail world I have focused on writing content for a variety of startups, but would now like to return to professional web development. To that end, I am completing a specialisation in this field on Coursera, and supplementing what I learn there with training on freeCodeCamp.


My HTML and CSS skills are quite strong, so I am applying most of my effort to JavaScript at the moment, before moving on to algorithms and back-end projects. The goal is for this very basic portfolio site to evolve as I strengthen and grow and my skill set.


Although I am also quite comfortable working in Photoshop and Illustrator, I am interested in exploring a career in web development, rather than web design.
