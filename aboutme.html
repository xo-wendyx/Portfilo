Take a look at some of my work...

In this project, my partner and I had to go through the design process of creating our own side scrolling game on Scratch, that had to connect to something we both liked and cared about. The first thing that we did was to identify the Minimum Viable Product for our game, and figuring out what features and story line that we wanted to do. The second step was to create a storyboard of our game, explaining the beginning, middle, and end of our game. The third step was to figured out what artwork we wanted and characters we wanted. We ended choosing the character Tom and Jerry, the objective of the game was that Jerry needed to avoid all of the obstacles that were coming his way, such as: Tom, cactus, bushes, and much more! Once, we had all our designing features, we began coding our game!

We were recently given an assignment, where each student needed to explain what the Core 4 values were. Girls Who Code, created the concept of the Core 4, because these concepts can be combined to solve many kinds of problems, such as when a person is building a game, creating artwork, and etc; the Core 4 consist of: variables, loops, conditionals, and functions. Our task was to look up the technical definitions of each word, a piece of code on python and scratch, and creating an analogy to describe these words. You can take a look at the examples that I created by clicking on the button that states “Google Docs”, this button will take you to the google doc that I created, describing the Core 4.
